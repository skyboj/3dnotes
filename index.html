Mind Palace

One-Pager for MVP: A 3D Space for Your Ideas

Problem

Current note-taking and document applications (Notion, Evernote, Obsidian) are effective, but limited to a two-dimensional space. They fail to leverage **human spatial memory** — one of the most powerful memorization mechanisms, known since Ancient Greece as the "method of loci" or "memory palace."

Our Solution

We are building an application that brings knowledge organization into an intuitive **3D space**. Users will be able to create "mind palaces" — virtual rooms where each note, document, or idea is placed as an object in the environment. This allows for the creation of visual and spatial connections between data, improving retention and information systematization.

MVP — Fast Launch and Hypothesis Validation

For rapid hypothesis validation and feedback collection, our MVP will focus on core functionality, ensuring **cross-platform compatibility** and high performance even on less powerful devices.

Key MVP Features:

  • 3D Space: One pre-designed minimalist room with navigation (walking, looking around).
  • Note Creation: Ability to create and edit notes in Markdown format.
  • Spatial Placement: Users can "pin" notes to walls and objects within the 3D scene.
  • Interaction: Simple selection and reading of notes on hover or click.
  • Performance: A web application optimized to run in a browser across most devices.

Target Audience

  • Students and researchers working with large volumes of study materials.
  • Writers, screenwriters, and creative professionals for organizing ideas.
  • Programmers and engineers for visualizing complex systems.
  • Anyone seeking more effective ways to learn and manage knowledge.

Business Model (Freemium)

Free Tier

Access to core functionality: two rooms (one large, one small) and unlimited notes. Ideal for exploration and personal use.

Premium Tier

Will include custom room creation, extended customization (textures, lighting, furniture), collaborative access, integrations, and advanced content types (videos, 3D models).
